About Us.
Meet the developers in Team Sagar!
Team Sagar is a development team based in Long Beach. The team is composed of five students who are currently attending California State University, Long Beach, who all share a common passion for code and Computer Science. Each member are currently in their 4th year of their undergraduate degree. With each of them having grown up in vastly different environments, their individual experiences allowed them to develop their own unique interests for the field of CECS. Having met in a UI/UX Design Course at CSULB, each of them met to form Team Sagar, which focuses on these following fields of development: Web and mobile application design/development, Game Development, UI/UX Design and Front End development.
What are we up to now?
Currently, Team Sagar hopes to expand their toolbox and gain more experience through completing projects that focus on the fields listed above. Having diverse interests amongst its members, Team Sagar strives to be successful as a well-rounded development team. In addition, each member are working on completing their undergraduate degree this year. Furthermore, Team Sagar is currently working on developing a web-based budgeting application that provides users with a free service for making smarter financial decisions and as of right now in the planning stage.
